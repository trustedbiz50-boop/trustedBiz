📍 Get on Google Maps

Getting on Google Maps is free and gets you more customers. Follow these steps:

1

Go to Google Business Profile

Visit business.google.com and sign in with your Google account.

2

Add Your Business

Click "Add your business to Google". Enter your business name and category exactly as on TrustedBiz.

3

Set Your Location

Pin your business on the map. Use the same coordinates you used on TrustedBiz for consistency.

4

Verify Your Business

Google will send a postcard or call to verify. Once verified, you appear in Google Maps searches.

5

Add Your TrustedBiz Website Link

In your Google Business Profile, add your TrustedBiz website link. This connects Google customers directly to your premium website.
