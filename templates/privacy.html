Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when registering an account or listing a business — such as your name, phone number, and business details. We also collect location data when you use the map features, only with your explicit permission.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to display your business listing, help customers find you via maps and WhatsApp, and improve our services. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Location Data

Location access is used only to help you pin your business on the map or find businesses near you. Location is never shared without your knowledge.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. Payments are processed through MTN MoMo or Airtel Money — we do not store payment credentials.

5. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us via WhatsApp or email listed on our home page.
